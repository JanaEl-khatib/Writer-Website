What are some tips to start writing?

  • Start Small: Begin with short pieces like flash fiction, a personal anecdote, or brief description of things that inspire you or interest you.
  • Find Your Voice: Experiment with different styles and genres, find what is most natural and authentic to you.
  • Keep a Journal: Capture your thoughts, observations, and ideas regularly.
  • Believe in Yourself: Writing is a journey of self-discovery and expression. Embrace the process, trust your instincts, and enjoy the creative journey.

How to overcome writer's block or self-doubt?

Developing strategies such as free-writing, setting achievable goals, or seeking support from writing communities can help navigate challenges.


How to improve writing skills?

Be ready to receive feedback! Don't be afraid to take criticism from people and apply those corrections in future works.
